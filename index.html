HELLO!

Hello, my name is Jacqueline Morales. I'm a Freelance Graphic Designer based in Silver Lake, California. I love making beautiful and creative designs.

I've had a passion for art ever since I was a young girl. Eventually, my passion for art soon grew into a passion for art & design. I graduated from Cal State Long Beach with a degree in Graphic Design and I have been doing graphic design for more than 10 years.

I am an enthusiastic, hardworking designer with a great eye for detail and a real passion for print and web design. I take great satisfaction in my ability to create clean and well thought-out designs.

Thank you for taking a look at my website. Please do not hesitate to contact me if you would like to work with me.
